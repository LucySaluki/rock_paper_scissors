Welcome to the Game Rock/Paper/Scissors

This is a simple game of chance (with possibly a bit of wit, if you can learn your opponants moves). Simple pick and option rock, paper or scissors. Each option has its strength and weakness.

Rock will win over scissors as it will blunt them, but will lose over paper as it is covered by that.

Paper will win over rock as it covers it, but will lose to paper as it is cut.

Scissors will win over paper as it cuts it, but will lose to rock as they are blunted by it.

NB: If you want to play against the computer enter "computer" as the second player name and dont choose drom the dropdown.

Choose Start to play the game
